Purpose of Vision and Mission

- Set direction
- Inspire people

## Vision

- Where do we want to be in the future?
- Where are we going?
- Why should we go there?
- What is winning?

## Mission

- How do we get to where we are going?
- Who do we do it for?
- Who is our main customer?

### Vision > Mission > Objectives > Strategies > Tactics
