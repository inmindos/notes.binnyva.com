This is a framework for continuous improvement and learning. It has for steps that can repeat...

- Plan: Understand the process, define the problem, schedule tasks
- Do: Execute, document results
- Check: Capture metrics, retrospect, compare results to expectations
- Act: Incorporate learnings, update process, communicate updates
