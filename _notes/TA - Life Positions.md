In [[Transactional Analysis]], these are the possible life positions you can hold...

- I am OK, You are OK (I+U+)
- I am not OK, You are OK (I-U+)
- I am OK, You are not OK (I+U-)
- I am not OK, You are not Ok (I-U-)
