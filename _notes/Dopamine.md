Dopomine is a Neurotransmitter.

Dopamine Rich Areas in the brain are...
- Hypothalamus
- Olfactory Bulb
- Retina

Many pathways to take it to the other areas.

Dopamine acts on G Protein-coupled receptors(5 types)

### Functions
- Movement
- Reward
- Attention
- Motivation
- Sleep Regulation
- Many, many more
